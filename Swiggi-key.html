Paid Customization Options

All the options below will increase the overall unit cost due to increased build time and/or material differences.

LED Colors + $5

The default LED color is white. I can also solder on green, blue, red, or orange LEDs. You can mix/match them however you want. There are four LEDs on the keypad: one under each switch, and one on either side of the device under the PCB (for side lighting).

Ceramic Finish + $20

Get the top and bottom stainless steel plates coated in a durable ceramic layer. Currently the only colors available are: forest green, deep purple, and jet blue. Contact me for other colors.

Engineered Debounce + $20

I will personally fine-tune a lower debounce for your particular switches. I can only guarantee that the debounce will be lower than 3 ms, not by how much, due to variations in switch production. You will receive a custom datasheet for your device showing a typical debounce cycle for your switches.

Fully Custom Unit
